## Life Lessons to Take Home

1. The God-sized hole in our heart **can only be filled by God**.

Galatians 6:7, 2 Samuel 11:1-12:14, Genesis 47:9

1 John 2:1-2, Romans 6:23, Colossians 2:13-14, Ephesians 2:3-5
