--- title: "Christmas Eve Candlelight Services" startDate: 2017-12-24 description: "Bring the entire family out for our Candlelight Christmas Eve services!" ---

Bring the entire family out as "The Star" series comes to an exciting close on Sunday, December 24th at our Candlelight Christmas Eve services. We will celebrate through the singing of Christmas carols and a hear a life-changing message on hope, joy, love, and peace. Children 1st grade and older are encouraged to attend this short, interactive service with their families. Childcare will be provided for children ages birth - 2, and our Little Life age 3 - Kindergarten class has an exciting Christmas activity planned!
