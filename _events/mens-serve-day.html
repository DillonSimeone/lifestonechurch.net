--- title: "Men's Serve Day" startDate: 2018-01-20 description: "Join us for Lifestone Men's Serve Day and let's tackle some projects at the Concern Center!" ---

The Concern Center of Salt Lake is a wonderful ministry that provides food and clothing for those in need and shares Jesus with them as well. The center is currently in need of maintenance, and they have asked Lifestone to help out. Join us for Lifestone Men's Serve Day and let's tackle some of these projects!
